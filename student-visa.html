Student visa FAQ

Real search-style questions students ask before applying abroad.

Who is a good student visa consultancy in Bangladesh?

Students usually look for a consultancy that can support country shortlisting, SOP review, scholarship direction, and visa-file readiness. NextBorder is structured around those exact needs.

When should I start my student visa process from Bangladesh?

It is better to start early so there is enough time for admission steps, tuition planning, bank documents, accommodation, visa paperwork, and embassy scheduling.

Which countries are good for low budget study in Europe?

Students often compare Italy, Malta, Romania, and Hungary when looking for more affordable education routes in Europe with scholarship potential.

Can you help with SOP, motivation letter, and document review?

Yes. NextBorder can support statement review, document consistency, and stronger application preparation before the visa stage.

Do you support students after they receive an offer letter?

Yes. Students often need help with next-step planning such as visa documents, tuition timing, accommodation preparation, and travel readiness.
